Wear a non-medical mask or cloth face covering to help stop the spread of COVID-19, especially if it’s not possible to consistently maintain a 2-metre physical distance from others. Wear your mask or cloth face covering safely and make sure it fits well.
In some jurisdictions, the use of masks in many indoor public spaces and on public transit is now mandatory. This will depend on the rate of infection or spread of COVID-19 in your community. Follow the advice of your local public health authority when it comes to wearing a non-medical mask or cloth face covering.
Nearly three in five Canadians – 58% – reported as of June 11 that they were regularly wearing face masks when out in public
Canadians are more likely than Americans to say they are avoiding crowded places during the pandemic. 76% of Canadians said this on June 11, while 63% of Americans said the same on June 22.
